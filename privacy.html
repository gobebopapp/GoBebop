Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Introduction

GoBebop ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at gobebop.app.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email Address: When you subscribe to our newsletter through the contact form
  • Feedback: When you submit feedback or contact us through our contact form

Information Automatically Collected

  • Location Data: If you enable location services in your browser, we collect your approximate location to show nearby kid-friendly locations and calculate distances. This data is only used during your session and is not stored permanently.
  • Usage Data: Through Google Analytics, we collect information about how you use our site, including pages visited, time spent, device type, and browser information.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for analytics and site functionality (see Cookie Policy below).

How We Use Your Information

  • Newsletter: To send updates about new features, locations, and improvements to GoBebop
  • Analytics: To improve our service and understand how users interact with our site
  • Location Services: To show you nearby locations and calculate distances from your current position
  • Customer Support: To respond to your feedback and questions

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties. We only share data with trusted service providers:

  • Mailchimp: For newsletter management and email delivery
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance monitoring
  • Mapbox: For interactive map functionality and location services

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Withdraw Consent: Disable location services or decline cookies
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our feedback form or email us directly.

Cookie Policy

We use cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

Required for site functionality. These cannot be disabled:

  • Cookie consent preferences
  • Welcome modal display status
  • Session management

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

Used to understand how visitors use our site. These require your consent:

  • Google Analytics cookies
  • Google Tag Manager

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through the cookie consent banner that appears on your first visit.

Data Retention

  • Newsletter Emails: Retained until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics Data: Retained according to Google Analytics default settings (26 months)
  • Location Data: Not stored permanently; only used during your active session
  • Feedback: Retained as long as necessary to respond and improve our service

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Our security measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure third-party service providers with strong privacy practices
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited data collection (we only collect what we need)

Children's Privacy

GoBebop is designed to help families find kid-friendly locations, but our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. When we transfer your data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

If we make material changes, we may notify you through our newsletter or by displaying a prominent notice on our website.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Website: gobebop.app
  • Feedback Form: Available on our website
  • Email: gobebopapp@gmail.com

Third-Party Services

For more information about how our third-party service providers handle your data:

  • Mapbox Privacy Policy: mapbox.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Analytics Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Mailchimp Privacy Policy: intuit.com/privacy/statement
  • Tally (Feedback Form) Privacy Policy: tally.so/help/privacy-policy

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: For analytics cookies and newsletter communications
  • Legitimate Interests: For site functionality, security, and service improvement
  • Contract: To provide the services you request (e.g., location-based features)
