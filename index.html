Discover the best activities, playgrounds, museums, cafes, and attractions for families with children in Amsterdam.

About GoBebop

GoBebop is an interactive map application designed to help families discover kid-friendly locations throughout Amsterdam. Whether you're looking for indoor activities on a rainy day, outdoor playgrounds for sunny weather, or family-friendly cafes and restaurants, GoBebop makes it easy to find the perfect spot for your children.

Our curated database includes hundreds of verified locations suitable for babies (0-2 years), toddlers and preschoolers (3-6 years), and big kids (7-12 years). Each location includes detailed information about facilities, entry fees, age appropriateness, and whether it's indoor or outdoor.

Features

  • Interactive map showing all kid-friendly locations in Amsterdam
  • Filter by age group: Babies (0-2), Preschoolers (3-6), Big Kids (7-12)
  • Filter by weather: Indoor activities for rainy days, Outdoor activities for sunny days
  • Filter by entry fee: Free activities or paid attractions
  • Filter by activity type: Active Play, Animals & Nature, Amusements & Attractions, Classes & Workshops, Culture & Learning, Restaurants & Cafes
  • Distance-based sorting when location services are enabled
  • Detailed information for each location including facilities (toilets, changing tables), accessibility, and seasonal availability
  • Direct navigation links to get directions via Google Maps
  • Mobile-optimized interface for on-the-go discovery

Activity Categories

Active Play

Playgrounds, climbing facilities, trampoline parks, sports centers, and other locations where kids can burn energy and develop physical skills.

Animals & Nature

Zoos, petting farms, nature centers, parks with wildlife, and outdoor spaces where children can learn about and interact with animals and nature.

Amusements & Attractions

Entertainment venues, theme attractions, interactive exhibits, and fun destinations designed specifically for children's enjoyment.

Classes, Workshops & Camps

Educational programs, skill-building workshops, art classes, music lessons, sports training, and holiday camps for children of all ages.

Culture & Learning

Museums, science centers, libraries, cultural centers, and educational attractions that combine fun with learning opportunities.

Restaurants & Cafes

Family-friendly dining establishments with kids' menus, play areas, and welcoming atmospheres for families with young children.

Location Information

Each location on GoBebop includes comprehensive details to help you plan your visit:

  • Age Suitability: Clear indicators showing which age groups (babies 0-2, preschoolers 3-6, big kids 7-12) will enjoy the location
  • Indoor/Outdoor: Information about whether activities are indoors (perfect for rainy days) or outdoors (ideal for sunny weather)
  • Entry Fees: Transparent information about free entry locations versus paid attractions
  • Facilities: Details about available amenities including toilets, changing tables, and accessibility features
  • Seasonal Information: Operating schedules and seasonal availability where applicable
  • Website Links: Direct links to official websites for more information and booking
  • Navigation: One-click directions via Google Maps

How to Use GoBebop

  1. Browse the Map: Explore locations visually on the interactive map of Amsterdam
  2. Apply Filters: Use the filter panel to narrow down options by age, weather, entry fee, or activity type
  3. View List: Switch to list view to see all locations with distance sorting (when location services enabled)
  4. Select a Location: Click any location to view detailed information including description, facilities, and contact details
  5. Get Directions: Use the navigation button to open Google Maps for turn-by-turn directions
  6. Share: Share interesting locations with friends and family using the share button
